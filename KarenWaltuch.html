Karen Waltuch

Violist

Karen Waltuch is from Arizona, a red state, where she was forced into a life of crime by her childhood violin teacher who, like the rest of the world, thought she was just too goddamn big to do anything productive: enter the viola.

An Haiku:

they eat my cat food.
scurry fast and make it home,
before i smoosh you.

On the turntable:

Lately and always she listens to Dolly Parton,
Meredith Monk,
Oprah Winfrey
and her conscience.
