"TubaJoe" Exley

Tubist and
Composer and
Musical Trouble Causer

TubaJoe is one of the busier tuba players in NYC, playing lots of gigs in lots of bands and despises "played with" lists. If you crave that info, it's best to go to one of his gigs and buy him a beer, then he might tell you, but then again maybe not. You might have to try buying him another beer, and maybe even another. TubaJoe writes music too sometimes, but only if he feels like it. He is currently working on a album which is a tribute to tribute albums.
