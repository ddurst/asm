ANTI-SOCIAL MUSIC presents:
WIP Free-for-all BBQ potluck!

Yeah, sorry.

You've probably had your fill of going to a new music ensemble's Works-in-Progress-Free-For-All-Potluck-BBQ. Seems it's that time of year, and everyone else has probably beaten us to it.

So it's with great shame that we, too, must doff our Serious Artist All-Black uniform in the name of that baby-shaking, hand-kissing thing.

Just bring whatever you brought to the last two new music potlucks. Something with substance; you know, like macaroni salad.

On the positive side, you get sneak peeks at ambitious new works by all your favorite composers all afternoon. Plus beer and brats!

Assuming someone brings beer.

Did we mention it's free? And it's not in Manhattan! You can hoot and holler instead of golf clapping.

Maybe not so much hollering.

Don't forget the macaroni salad!

Please don't everyone bring macaroni salad.

Sunday, June 29, 2014
noon until... ?
320 Stratford Road in Ditmas Park BK (Q to Cortelyou)
FREE

In no particular order (but possibly in order of best food contribution):

JAMES BARRY - HUSTLE for string quartet

MAX DUYKERS - "Perch" for Bass Clarinet, two MIDI Foot Controllers, and Bar Chimes

JEFF HUDGINS - Mannequin Heart for String Sextet

BARRY SEROFF - What's a title? flute solo suite

ALLA ZAGAYKEVYCH - Voice/Way (2013) for Cello, Piano, Violin, Clarinet, Flute and electronics

This program is supported, in part, by public funds from the New York City Department of Cultural Affairs, in partnership with the City Council, and in part with public funds from the New York State Council on the Arts, a state agency.
