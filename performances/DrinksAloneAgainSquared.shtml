ASM Drinks Alone (Squared)

Drinks Alone is a series ASM began a decade ago (!) to feature solo pieces (one performer, not necessarily just one instrument).

Here we are now, a pre-pubescent series: all disproportionate, outgrowing our shoes, hormones bubbling, and still searching for a date to the dance.

Well, fear not, gentle listener. We've matured much in this time. Though there have been but seven performances in this series, we're hitting our stride. We know what you want before you know you want us to know what you want, and we're getting good at getting the goods, if you know what I mean. You know? Good.

Anyway, we continue to find like-minded lost souls who just want to be understood. And so it shall come to pass that alone and drinking as a group again will grace a stage in your fair city. Maybe if you're not busy, you could come out, hang out, sit down, throw down, drink up, listen up. We can do it together.

And yet so very, very alone.
