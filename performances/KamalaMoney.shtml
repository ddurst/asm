ASM Gives
Kamala
Money

Tuesday, 11/24
doors 7:30
$15
HERE Art Center
145 6th Ave
C/E to Spring Street.

We at ASM have long dreamed of seeing Pat Muchmore take his rightful place as the premiere countertenor of the postclassical world. Now, that dream is within our grasp, which is why we're helping Kamala Sankaram raise money for her chamber opera. ASM will be playing a special set at Kamala's benefit next Tuesday, 11/24 at HERE Art Center (145 6th avenue). For only $15, you'll get to hear ASM (with music by Muchmore, Lasaga, La Rose, Brittelle, and Wriggle), Gamelan- Son of Lion, and Otis Pike and the Smokey Mirrors; you'll also get free food, free wine, and birthday cake (did we mention it's Kamala's birthday?!) Please come out and help us put Pat in his rightful place!
