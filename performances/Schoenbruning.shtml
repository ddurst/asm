Diana's Opening Night

We're looking forward to enjoying and celebrating Brooklyn-based Diana Schoenbrun's solo show! Featuring original artwork, illustrations, and puppets... and us on opening night!

Join us for drink specials, complimentary hors d'oeuvres, and the riveting, orchestral adventure in miniature that is ASM music trio, starting at 8 pm at Red Horse Cafe.

The ASM music trio features Pat Muchmore, Bradley Kemp, and Ed RosenBerg III performing five world premieres and two ASM premieres!

About longtime ASM friend Diana Schoenbrun: Diana Schoenbrun is an illustrator, author, and puppet builder based in Brooklyn, New York. Diana enjoys working in a variety of mediums such as acrylic, paper, fabric, and wood. Her work ranges from humorous to whimsical. Diana has published two craft books for children and adults called, Beasties and Puppet Play. Her children's book illustrations have appeared in many educational books in the US. Diana has also enjoyed teaching workshops at Etsy, Makerfaire, American Folk Art Museum in NYC, the Museum of Craft and Folk Art San Francisco, and 826NYC. She created puppets for a music video and for the Disney movie, The Muppets(2011). Diana recently returned from Prague where she was studying marionette carving and performance. She is currently working on a collaborative writing, music, and illustration project.
