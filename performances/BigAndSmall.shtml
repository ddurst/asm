Big Pieces For Small Groups

Due to technical difficulties, we had to postpone our January 17 show. The new date is February 23.

Anti-Social Music will play a night of world premieres with a catch: only small ensembles, and no piece is over seven minutes long. So, it's "big" in the sense that it's momentous, and "small" in the sense that they are more focused, svelte works and groups.

There will be WORLD PREMIERES by Dan Lasaga, Pat Muchmore, Ed Rosenberg, Charles Waters, and John Wriggle!

Ensembles consisting of cello, clarinet, saxophone, violin, and piano!

Sunday, February 23, 2014 8 pm
Greenwich House Music School
46 Barrow Street, New York, NY 10014

We promise we won't postpone this one, because we have a busy Spring coming up.

Performed by the attractive, educated, and dextrous Hubert Chen, Jean Cook, Domenica Fossati, Dan Lasaga, Pat Muchmore, Ed Rosenberg, and Charles Waters!

This program is supported, in part, by public funds from the New York City Department of Cultural Affairs, in partnership with the City Council, and in part with public funds from the New York State Council on the Arts, a state agency.
