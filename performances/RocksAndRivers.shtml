Rocks and Rivers

Rocks and Rivers follows up Heart Songs and Sounds from 2022. It's a new cycle of songs and compositions exploring the qualities of human emotion as they relate to our natural world, performed by the same trio.

Performing:
Theresa Rosas, piano and vocals
Alan Grubner, violin
Sam Weber, bass

Friday, November 8th, 2024 at 7:00 PM
at Renee Weller Concert Hall
