New York's Alright If You Like Saxophones

STREAM/DOWNLOAD THIS SHOW (ish)

The trick with premieres shows is that they usually happen once, and then we only maybe will get some of those premiered pieces into our regular repertoire. Plus, we only do those shows in New York. And New York is terrible.

But New York's alright if you like saxophones, and fortunately this show is not only an encore performance of our most recent premieres show, but also ALL SAXOPHONES, ALL THE TIME.

Tuesday, March 31, 2015 at 9:30pm
ShapeShifter Lab
18 Whitwell Pl, Brooklyn NY 11215
D/N/R to Union St

ENCORE PRESENTATION

Awkwardly priced at $8

Featuring the multi-talented, not to mention attractive saxophonists:
Jeremy Udden (soprano and alto),
Josh Sinton (alto and baritone),
Evan Rapport (alto and tenor),
Ed RosenBerg (tenor and bass),
Jeff Hudgins (soprano, alto, and baritone),
and Alex Hamlin (alto and tenor).

Program:

DAVID DURST - fraud! for saxophone quartet

JEFF HUDGINS - bike for saxophone quartet

ANDREA LA ROSE - Zwiefache — jenny, the dirndl queen of ghana for four alto saxophones with thunder tubes

DAN LASAGA - Algorhythmic Buggery for saxophone quartet

PAT MUCHMORE - Fr.XIV.a–d for saxophone quintet

ED ROSENBERG - Sculpting and Animating His Floral Children for saxophone quartet

KAMALA SANKARAM - shut it down for saxophone quartet
