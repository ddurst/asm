Summer's here and there's nothing we at ASM like better than a cold beer and a refreshing dip in the waters of Brooklyn. Ahhh, the historic Gowanus Canal, the placid Prospect Park lake, and, of course, the damp coolness of Union Pool. Join us as we toast the summer with our friends the Debutante Hour (good girls lured astray by the power of the accordion) and Jerseyband (more raucous than Jersey Shore, and with bigger hair). Last one in's a rotten egg!

This program is supported, in part, by public funds from the New York City Department of Cultural Affairs, in partnership with the City Council, and in part with public funds from the New York State Council on the Arts, a state agency. Which is basically to say - your tax dollars at work. Yeah, frightening isn't it?
