ASM Presents Invocation XII

Invocation pairs installation artists, improvisers, and performers across all disciplines to collectively synthesize a durational work unique to each iteration, conjuring the collective ethos in a single, uninterrupted performance.

Invocation XII will feature a sound installation and performance by percussionist & sound artist Lisa Cameron, a commission by renowned composer/improviser & instrument builder id m theftable, and an electronics performance by Dominic Coles.

Friday, December 8th, 2023 @ 8:00PM
