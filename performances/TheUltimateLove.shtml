ASM Presents
 
The Ultimate Love

In these times of one hand pulling the other back and forth and left and right and flipping oneself over, then throwing onself into the ropes only to hit oneself over the head with a chair, we here at ASM headquarters have just the thing for you:

A suite of songs inspired by (and in some cases built around) the WWF wrestling promos of The Ultimate Warrior, presented as an imaginary tag-team event with The Ultimate Warrior & Sir Cubworth joining forces.

Songs include:
I Live Intravenously
Interference in my Pathways
Now is the Time
This is Happening
Is It Lonely
The Frustration of Mankind
and more!

Composed & Performed by Ed RosenBerg III
