ASM Presents
 
Amen: Joy Guidry Trio

Joy Guidry's newest album, AMEN, was released on May 10th, 2024. On August 25th, the Joy Guidry Trio will be playing an album release show.

You don't get many (any?) chances to see live experimental gospel jazz ambient spiritual soul featuring a bassoonist. Guidry does not disappoint, whatever your expectations are, and more importantly, it's badass.

Here at ASM we're just putting the facts out there: you won't hear anything else like this. In this remix culture, seeing a human perform in person is getting more and more rare. Sure, you could sit at home and listen to it, counting off another day that you whirled through space in a continuum of Blursdays, or you could step out into the golden light of a Sunday and immerse yourself in the power of community. By the end, you too will be saying Amen!
