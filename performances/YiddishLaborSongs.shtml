Bop Kabbalah + Voices: New Radical Yiddish Liberation Songs
Judith Berkson / Ty Citerman / Sara Serpa

original contemporary settings of radical Yiddish labor songs and poems for voices, guitar, piano and electronics - shedding light on the century-old fight against capitalism for a truly just and equitable world

Ganavya & Rajna
