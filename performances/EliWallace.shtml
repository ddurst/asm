Anti-Social Music

Past Performance

ASM
and
Eli Wallace
Present

With the pent-up frustrations from years of being shut down by public health emergencies, it’s reasonable to expect that artists will appear with the fruits of years of indulging their creative whims.

Wait, no, that’s wrong.

Artists gonna do what artists gonna do. And what you want is a spectacular show. You don’t want all the fiddly-fiddly of some meticulously balanced, Rube-Goldbergian sonic domino trail, no. You want raw art.

Well, that’s what you get.

ASM and Eli Wallace present for you, one night only, and never to be repeated!
