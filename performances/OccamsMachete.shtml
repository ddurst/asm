ASM Presents
 
Elliot Sharp SysOrk’s
 
Occam’s Machete

Coming October 12, 2023 at MAIN DRAG MUSIC, Brooklyn: Anti-Social Music presents Elliott Sharp SysOrk performing Occam’s Machete for 24 strings plus the world premiere of Nero Code for four contrabasses.

Occam’s Machete is a 50-minute microtonal string orchestra piece for 24 players. Main Drag has a wonderful performance space with high ceilings and a spectacular sound, especially for acoustic instruments. The musicians will be situated throughout the space for an immersive sonic experience.

The orchestra will consist of:

VIOLIN: Shaleah Feinstein, Sarah Thomas, Ledah Finck, Sarah Bernstein, On Ka'a Davis, Aimee Neimann, Tom Chiu, Rachel Golub, Joanna Mattrey, Sana Nagano, Jean Cook.

VIOLA: Ron Lawrence, Jessica Pavone, Judith Insell, Amy Huimei Tan, John Pickford Richards.

CELLO: Hao Jiang, Irene Han, Alex Waterman, Chris Otto.

CONTRABASS: JA Andrew Wilhite-Hannisdal, Shayna Dulberger, Kevin Ray, Kyle Motl.

Thursday, October 12, 2023 @ 8PM
Main Drag Music
50 South 1st Street, Williamsburg (between Kent Ave and Wythe Ave)
