OASIS GRAB BAG

Hey you!

Your favorite guerilla chamber group is doing a short set as part of the Chashama art organization's OASIS!! multimedia festival. Admission is free and we're performing Saturday night, May 11 at 7:30 in Chashama's storefront at 135 W. 42nd St. just west of Times Square.

It'll be a grab-bag program, featuring Ken Thomson's Don't Stop (NYC, 7 yrs), the third movement of Dan Lasaga's Vicious Cycles, and the final movement of Franz Nicolay's each today is yesterday's tomorrow (for moondog) as performed by the crackerjack ASM Players. For more info, check out www.chashama.org.

And stay tuned for the third ASM Premieres show June 21st at CAMI Hall, featuring all new works from the ASM composers and some newcomers. More to come...

For more information, e-mail info@antisocialmusic.org.
