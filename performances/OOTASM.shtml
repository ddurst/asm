OOTASM!!!!

(It's a new word for us, so we're practicing sounding it out.)

What is OOTASM, you say? Why, it's that state of deep ecstasy achieved through the tantric merging of Opera On Tap and Anti-Social Music. First, ASM kicks it ol' skool as the back-up band for OOT. (Kinda like the Pips, but less debonair.) Then OOT keeps it real singing ASM's greatest vocal hits. Now that's what I call music! Join us at Galapagos for opera, beer, and new musical heights. OO!
