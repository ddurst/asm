ANTI-SOCIAL MUSIC at the Safe-T Gallery on Sunday, April 18th at 4 PM!! Only $6.99!

OK, we're not feeling overly creative just yet, but that's just the prose! The music will be great as always, including old smash hits like John Wriggle's 100 Grand a Year, Pat Muchmore's Fracture II (by popular demand!), and Andrea LaRose's breakbeat. We'll also have Ken Thomson's recent smash hit, Song, and the premiere of a brand-new Peter Hess proto-smash hit called Voiceprint.

Safe-T-Gallery is located at 134 Bayard St. between Graham and Manhattan Avenues, on the border of Williamsburg and Greenpoint, Brooklyn. This is about a quarter-of-a-mile from either the L train Graham stop or the G train Nassau stop. For more complete instructions check out their website.
