Glue Gun Optimism + ASM

Anti-Social Music and Ed RosenBerg's Glue Gun Optimism will be performing separately.... and then together.

Anti-Social Music will play a Whitmans's sampler of duo and trio madness, featuring Jean Cook (violin), Hubert Chen (violin), Pat Muchmore (Cello), Brad Kemp (Contrabass), Ed Rosenberg (various reeds).

  • Concerto for Anyone by ... anyone!
  • selections from Fr.V.a–m by Ed and Pat!
  • Fr.VI.a–j by Brad and Pat!
  • A trio of Brad Kemp's by Brad, Pat, and Ed!
  • A duet of Ed's by Pat and Ed!
  • Possibly Vafþruðnismal if Pat and Hu want to do it!
  • And concluding with ASM joining GGO for a group rendition of Ed's piece Gore!

Glue Gun Optimism, featuring James Hirschfeld, Alex Wyatt, Simon Jermyn, Landon Knoblock and Ed RosenBerg will play selections from their debut album, The Warming, as well as several brand new compositions.

Sunday, December 15, 2013
7-8 pm: ASM
8-9 pm: GGO
9:30 sharp: show will end!

Spectrum
121 Ludlow St, Second Floor
New York, NY 10002
