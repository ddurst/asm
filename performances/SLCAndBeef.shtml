ASM kicks the knowledge to the kids at college!

On Wednesday March 29th at 8 PM, relive none of your college glory days with ASM as they show the wee ones at Sarah Lawrence College how to rawk the house, whilst remaining true to the pure, untainted ideals of Enlightenment Era concert music. Ehem... Maybe.

ASM has a beef with a rat!

Well, actually, no, we don't. Sorry for the confusion. But we are premiering the first act of Gordon Beeferman's new opera The Rat Land on April 1st at the Improvised or Otherwise festival! Low winds will growl, high winds will trill, strings will moan, and everyone will be caught up in microtonal mayhem, as we spin a tale of a dysfunctional American family. Not to be missed!
