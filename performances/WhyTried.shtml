The Why

A night of original music! You want details, and you want to go out on a Wednesday night. (It’s OK, we all do.) DO IT. You only live once. We think. (Actually, we think time is an illusion, the soul is eternal, and “life” as we speak of it is a continuum of various states of being. But we can’t prove this, so go see live music while you can.)

The Why: is Anders Nilsson (electric guitar), Jeremy Carlstedt (drums), and special guest On Ka'a Davis (electric guitar)

The what: a double bill of original music with Huvudbry

The where: Mama tried, 787 3rd Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11232

The when: Wednesday, September 13, 2023 at 7:30pm

The who: The Why, we already covered that

The how: D/N/R/W to 25th St and then walk a block. Look, it’s not hard.
