ASM Presents
 
Oracle Bones

+ Cecilia Lopez (solo)

Oracle Bones is collaborative quartet featuring Drew Wesely (nylon string guitar), Joe Moffett (trumpet), Zosha Warpeha (hardanger fiddle), and Carlo Costa (vibraphone/percussion).

Formed initially as a subset of the ongoing large ensemble project Living Sound Lab, the ensemble employs highly elastic compositional techniques to explore collectively interdependent timbral possibilities in this unique instrumentation.

For this particular presentation, Oracle Bones will be playing a synthesis of new works from each of the members melded together through improvisation and group composition.

This performance also features a solo set by Cecilia Lopez.
