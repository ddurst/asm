New Music Bake Sale

New Music Bake Sale
Irondale Center
Saturday, Sept. 25
85 South Oxford Street
Fort Greene, Brooklyn

...from 4:30-11:30pm, with performances by Anti-Social Music, Todd Reynolds, Newspeak, Wet Ink, Kathleen Supove, Dither, and many more. ASM will have a table and will be auctioning off some sweet ASM prizes.

What is the New Music Bake Sale? A delicious new event featuring live performances, bountiful beverages, merchandise, revelry, buffoonery, tomfoolery, and of course plenty of baked goods. It's all to benefit your favorite up-and-coming New York New Music Ensembles. More info at newmusicbakesale.org.
