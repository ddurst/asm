ASM Presents
 
ASM waters the garden

ASM Presents longtime partner composer Charles Waters in collection of new and revised garden works. Two quarters in contrasting moods and original long-form piece “Red Shed Garden” for flute and bass clarinet. Presented as part of ongoing series curated by Waters now in its sixth season Citimusic @ Red Shed.

ASM waters the garden
Sunday, May 15, 2022 @ 2-4pm
Red Shed Garden
266 Skillman Ave, Brooklyn 11211

Program:

I. PEACE FORMS FOUR (2011) (ST MARKS CHURCH)

II. NU GARDEN PARTICLES (2017, 2022)

III. RED SHED GARDEN (2019/2022)

Featuring:
Mary Cherney & Robert Michel - flutes
Peter Hess & Charles Waters - clarinets
