I was having a great conversation with a tree the other day, when this butterfly with a waxed moustache fluttered by saying, "It's time to come up with a title and announcement for your May show."

Way to trounce all over my vibe, dude! Jeez...

So I tried to pick up the conversation with the tree where we left off, but the leaves grew mohawks, played sad tunes on their cellos, and said, "Dammit! What are we calling the May 12 show?!"

Aw man, this trip was going downhill fast. The world started spinning and the last thing I remember were little eighth notes dancing around my head, chanting, "Bucolic Wonderland! Bucolic Wonderland!"

Duuuuude. Never smoking that stuff again...
