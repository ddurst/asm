Amy Kohn

Soprano and
Pianist

AMY KOHN In her mind, ASM stands for Amy's Supersexiest Musixperience! She's sung very high notes and played very big chords with ASM. She also leads the 7-piece AMY KOHN BAND which Peter Hess (bandmate) calls "Wonderfully Bizarro Prokofiev Pop." She semi-embodies Madonna singing her Like A Virgin arrangement with the pigtail-wearin' all-girl Main Squeeze Accordion Orchestra, which she assistant conducts when their German Leader is out of town. She also writes musicals about bathtubs and guys who get off on watching houses burn. Her 2nd CD: I'm in Crinoline, is due out early 2005, and her first, THE GLASS LAUGHS BACK is available for listening pleasure.
