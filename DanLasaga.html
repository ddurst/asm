Dan Lasaga

Composer

Dan Lasaga began studying music at age nine. Quite a few years later, he began jazz composition studies at NYU in 1995. He eventually studied under classical composer Marc Consoli, becoming more interested in classical genres. Dan received a Bachelor's of Music in 1999. Since then he has been working predominantly on classical chamber pieces incorporating jazz, world music and whatever else he can manage jam into it. Dan currently resides in Greenpoint, Brooklyn.
