John Wriggle

Composer and
Trombonist

John Wriggle is a founding member of punk-orchestra collective Anti-Social Music and soul-jazz-funk band The Ex Caminos, and has led projects ranging from the NYC new music ensemble The 11-Tet to the John Wriggle Swing Orchestra. He has also worked as a music editor for Hollywood films (Lord of the Rings trilogy), and in the the fields of music publishing and music production. He currently teaches music history at the City University of New York.
