Tim Byrnes

Trumpeter

Tim Byrnes (trumpet) has been playing music in New York City since the Fall of 1998 when he entered New York University pursuing an undergraduate degree in Jazz Performance and Composition (he finished that degree in a smooth 4 years). Since arriving, he has performed and/or recorded with (in rough chronological order) Candiria, Mr. Bungle's Trevor Dunn, Charlie Looker's Lavender, Time of Orchids, and of course Anti-Social Music. He has also led a plethora of his own composition-oriented projects including Budd Dwyer, Romance, Doppelganger, and his most current band, Friendly Bears. Friendly Bears play dissonant math-rock and jazz fusion.
